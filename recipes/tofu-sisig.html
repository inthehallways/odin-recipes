Tofu Sisig

Description

Lorem Ipsum.

Ingredients

  • 4 Blocks Tofu
  • 1 pc Onion
  • 2 pcs. Green Chilis
  • 1 1/2 pack Lady's Choice Mayonnaise (80mL)
  • 2 tbsp Soy Sauce
  • 2 1/2 tbsp Knorr Liquid Seasoning
  • 1 1/2 tbsp Calamansi Juice
  • 2 tbsp Oyster Sauce
  • 3 tbsp brown sugar
  • Pepper
  • A bit of Butter or Margarine (Optional)

Steps

  1. Cut the blocks of tofu into 3 slices each and fry them until crispy.
  2. Chop the onions and chilis, then set them aside.
  3. In a bowl, mix the mayonnaise, soy sauce, liquid seasoning, calamansi juice, oyster sauce, brown sugar, and pepper. Set them aside.
  4. Cut the fried tofu into small blocks.
  5. In a pan where you fried the tofu, sauté the onions until it becomes transluscent.
  6. Add the sauce you mix earlier then wait for it to darken in color.
  7. Add the chopped fried tofu and chilis, and sauté for a few minutes.
  8. Add butter or margarine and mix well. Best served hot.
